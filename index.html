About Us

Our Story

The East Kilbride Art Club had it’s first trial run at the start of 2018. We had such a successful few months that we are continuing on with the new session which begins this August, every Tuesday from 7-9pm in the Murray Owen Centre.

The club is open to all who are interested in drawing and painting and has members from beginners to professionals working in a variety of media. We have a range of activities lined up for this year including demonstrations from local artists, member run workshops and events. There will also be plenty of work nights, allowing you time to work on personal projects and portfolios.

If you are interested in becoming a member please fill out the form below to book your place for a free trial week. We look forward to seeing you soon!
