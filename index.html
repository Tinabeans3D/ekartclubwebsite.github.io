About Us

Our Story

The East Kilbride Art Club had it’s first trial run at the start of 2018. We had such a successful few months that we are continuing on with the new session which begins this August, every Tuesday from 7-9pm in the Murray Owen Centre.

We are a friendly club and open to all who are interested in drawing and painting. It’s a great place to work on and improve your skills and we welcome beginners to professionals, working in a variety of media.

There are lots of activities lined up for this year including demonstrations from local artists, member run workshops and events. There will also be plenty of work nights, allowing you time to work on personal projects and portfolios.

If you are interested in becoming a member please contact us using the details below to book your place for a free trial week. We look forward to seeing you soon!

Painting Day at Culzean Castle

Some of our members are taking part in the National Trust for Scotland’s "Capture Culzean" challenge on the 8th of September. Our members will be painting 'en plein air' and trying to create a piece of art in a day…come rain or shine!

On Sunday 9th September in the Stone Barn there will be a special one-day public exhibition and judging will take place at noon, followed by a prize giving.

The cost of the event is £15.00 which includes entry to the competition, admission to the country park on Saturday 8 September and admission to the country park for the competing artist and one guest on Sunday 9 September.

First prize: £100 plus a luxury overnight stay for two at the Eisenhower Hotel, Culzean Castle

Second prize: £50 plus afternoon tea for two at Culzean Castle

People’s Choice award: Afternoon tea for two at Culzean Castle

Winners will also have the opportunity to have their winning artwork exhibited at the Coach House Gallery (opposite the castle) for a full week following the competition.
